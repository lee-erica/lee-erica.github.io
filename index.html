Erica Lee

My name is Erica Lee and I was born and raised in Boulder, Colorado. I am currently in my fourth and final year at the University of Colorado Boulder pursuing an undergraduate degree in Information Science with a minor in Business. I am currently the Recruitment Advisor for the CU Boulder chapter of the Phi Sigma Pi National Honor Fraternity and a Student Ambassador for the College of Media, Communication, and Information.

Motivated by my curiosity in the growing amount of data that surrounds us, I hope to end up in a career centered around data analytics. My goal is to understand the changes our technologically-dependent society is undergoing and help us adapt to the new, data-driven world being built around us. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, listening to music, playing video games, and hiking.
