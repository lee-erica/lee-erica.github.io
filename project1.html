USING K-MEANS CLUSTERING ON MY SPOTIFY DATA



INFO 4604 - APPLIED MACHINE LEARNING
fall 2020


Research Question: How similar were my favorite songs released in 2020?

For the final project, we were required to use a machine learning method to analyze and predict our own set of data. I chose to use my own Spotify data and create a playlist of 200 songs that came out in 2020. I then used a k-means clustering method to group together similar tracks based on their audio features (danceability, energy, and valence). Ultimately, my model returned six clusters, or groups of similar songs.
