Version 2


For the second verison of my chart, I added a border using the borderWidth and borderColor properties. I also removed the background grid by removing the scales property, so there are less unnecessary lines to distract you from the chart. I lowered the opacity of the background colors by setting the backgroundColor property's last argument to 0.35, so that it is easier to see the two distinct areas. Lastly, I added a title to explain what the chart is representing by adding the title configuration to the options section.


Version 3


For my final version, I decided to change the size and colors of the dots so they were easier to see. I did this by using the pointBackgroundColor and pointRadius properties. To improve the interactivity of the chart, I changed the hover interactions. By increasing pointHoverRadius, the size of the points increases when you hover over it, telling you that you're looking at a specific point. I also added pointHoverBackgroundColor and pointHoverBorderColor to show the point with a darker opacity when you are hovering over it with your mouse. Lastly, I increased the legend's font size in the options property.
